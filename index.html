About Us

Daous Company was founded by Ibrahim Daou and Yahya DaouAbout Us Daous Company is an international fashion brand with headquarters in Germany and Morocco. Founded by Ibrahim Daou and Yahya Daou, we are a young and passionate team dedicated to creating high-quality T-shirts at fair prices. Our mission is to combine modern design, premium materials, and ethical production to bring you stylish and comfortable apparel. Every piece we create reflects our commitment to quality and affordability, ensuring that fashion is accessible to everyone. Join us on our journey to redefine casual wear with authenticity, innovation, and global inspiration.
